Foundations in Labor and Tenacity

My formative years in the labor industry were not just about the daily grind; they were the proving grounds for resilience and determination. Amidst the echoing din of a work site, I learned the irreplaceable value of hard work—a lesson carried through relentless 55+ hour weeks. Balancing the demands of the labor with the rigor of coding school wasn't easy, but it taught me the true meaning of commitment, paving the way for a future I'm determined to build with my own hands.

Embracing Challenges: Coding and Climbing

When I'm not solving physical puzzles in my work boots, I'm scaling rock faces or delving into the digital world of coding and 3D modeling. These arenas may seem worlds apart, yet they share a common thread—the joy of creating and the thrill of solving complex problems. Through years of tireless learning and practice, all while managing a demanding work schedule, I've harnessed the same focus and tenacity for my transition into the tech world. Now, standing at the precipice of a career change, I'm as ready to navigate the challenges of software development as I am a rugged bouldering route or a new country's horizon.
